Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

PlacementPro is an independent, privately-operated platform and is not affiliated with KIIT University. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use PlacementPro, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name and email address provided at signup
  • Usage data: questions solved, login activity, feature usage, and progress tracking, used to personalize and improve your experience
  • Payment confirmation: proof of payment (such as a screenshot) submitted for manual verification — we do not collect or store your card, UPI PIN, or banking credentials at any point, as payments are processed through external UPI/payment channels not controlled by the Platform
  • Feedback and communications: any feedback, support messages, or conversations submitted through the Platform, including the AI Mentor chat feature

2. How Your Data Is Stored and Protected

Your data security is a priority. All user data is stored in a secured, access-controlled database. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are cryptographically hashed using industry-standard hashing algorithms before storage, meaning even the Platform's own team cannot view your original password. Access to the underlying infrastructure is restricted to the Platform's core team only.

While we implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, no online system can guarantee absolute security. You acknowledge that you provide your information at your own risk, and the Platform shall not be liable for unauthorized access resulting from circumstances beyond our reasonable control.

3. Email Verification — Important Disclosure

The Platform does not currently send a confirmation email or OTP to verify that the email address provided at signup actually belongs to the registering user. This means account creation relies on self-declared, unverified information. By registering, you acknowledge this limitation and agree that the Platform is not responsible for any consequence resulting from the use of an unverified or third-party email address, including but not limited to disputes over account ownership.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and provide access to paid features
  • To track your learning progress, including solved questions, streaks, and leaderboard standing
  • To respond to support requests, feedback, and account-related queries
  • To analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve the Platform
  • To send essential account notifications, such as access activation confirmations

We do not use your personal data for third-party advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

To operate the Platform, we rely on trusted third-party infrastructure providers, including cloud hosting services, database hosting providers, code-execution services (for the online compiler feature), and AI language model providers (for the Mentor chat feature). These providers may process limited data (such as code submissions or chat messages) strictly to deliver their respective functionality, and are bound by their own privacy and security practices.

6. AI Mentor Conversations

If you use the AI Mentor feature, your messages and the mentor's responses are stored so that the mentor can maintain context across sessions (remembering your progress and prior conversations). This data is used solely to improve your learning experience within the Platform and is processed through a third-party AI service provider to generate responses.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide our Services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us; such requests will be processed within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by the Platform. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the details provided on the Platform.

9. Children's Privacy

This Platform is intended for college students and is not directed at, nor knowingly used to collect data from, children under the age of 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Continued use of the Platform after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, concerns, or data deletion requests, please reach out through the contact details provided on the Platform.
